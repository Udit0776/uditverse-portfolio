# Interactions

## Controls

W → Move Up

A → Move Left

S → Move Down

D → Move Right

E → Interact

ESC → Close Dialog

---

## Interaction Types

### Information Interaction

Used for:

- Signs
- Books
- Portraits

Result:

Opens information panel.

---

### Project Interaction

Used for:

- Project Stations
- Exhibits
- Dashboards

Result:

Opens detailed project modal.

---

### Achievement Interaction

Used for:

- Trophies
- Banners
- Milestones

Result:

Shows achievement information.

---

### Future Vision Interaction

Used for:

- Telescope
- Future Wall

Result:

Displays future goals and roadmap.
